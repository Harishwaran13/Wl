Holiday Ideas for Popular Destinations

Manali

Major Tourist Attractions:Solang Valley, Hidimba Devi Temple and Manali Sanctuary
Best Things To Do: Trying adventure activities and Strolling on Mall Road

Best Time To Visit: October to February

Ladakh

Major Tourist Attractions: Shanti Stupa, Tso Moriri Lake, Leh Palace, Nubra Valley, Namgyal Tsemo Gompa, Hall of Fame Museum and Spituk Monastery

Best Things To Do: Camping near Pangong Lake, Explore the Himalayan views, White water rafting in Zanskar River and Drive up till the Magnetic Hill

Best Time To Visit: June to September

Andaman

Major Tourist Attractions: Radhanagar Beach, Mahatma Gandhi Marine National Park, Viper Island, Chidiya Tapu Beach, Mount Harriet National Park
Best Things To Do: Explore the beaches, go for water sports like scuba diving, kayaking and snorkelling
Best Time To Visit: All year round
